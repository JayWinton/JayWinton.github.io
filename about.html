Dani has held a passion for the hair and beauty industry for most of her adult life. Has been licensed for six years in the state of Kansas. Mastered in a wide range of hair techniques including curly/thick hair cutting, long hair, and short styles. However Dani’s specialty and passion is color. Using a range of high quality products from Wella, including the amazing Blondor bleach, semi/demi/ and permanent colors as well. With the belief of continual education Dani has been able to stay current and at the forefront in the field of knowledge. Including new products and services. Different and alternative methods, and detail and research of the products being used on the scalp. With specializing in cutting edge color techniques, she is able to blend and design custom colors to suite your skin-tone, changing environments, and overall design goals with a consultation to give you the confidence with your new style! Specialties include highlights,lowlights,all over color, grey coverage/reduction,fashion cuts, short styles, men’s cut and style, American Crew certified. Dani places high value on the client and their satisfaction. A week-guaranteed for a haircut, and a week for all color services.
