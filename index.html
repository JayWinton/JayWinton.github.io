BLG Recycling LLC, A Kansas City area based company. Buying, selling and hauling away (Kansas City area) post-industrial plastics used for clean, top-quality products. International company who contracts with factories around the United States, to buy and recover plastic materials that can be scraped and repurposed into clean plastic products.

In today's world, plastics play an important part to our lives. The versatility allows for plastics to be used in car parts, to water bottles. With so many uses, and many more needed everyday, plastic recycling becomes a vital part. In 2015 there were 39.9 billion pounds of plastics (AAC-American Chemistry Council) That was reclaimed and recycled last year, with projections to go up through 2020 and beyond.

At BLG Recycling LLC, you can trust our international company to reduce commercial waste, in a industrial, efficient, hazard-free process designed to simplify the logistics of plastic recycling. BLG Recycling is the smart, and dynamic plastic recycling resource and trading company you need for your professional services of recycled plastics.
